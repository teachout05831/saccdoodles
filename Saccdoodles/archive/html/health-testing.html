Health Testing

Our commitment to breeding healthy, happy puppies through comprehensive health testing.

Why Health Testing Matters

At SACC Doodles, we believe that responsible breeding starts with comprehensive health testing. Before any of our dogs enter our breeding program, they undergo extensive health screenings to ensure they are free from genetic conditions that could be passed on to their puppies.

Our commitment to health testing means that when you bring home a SACC Doodle puppy, you can have confidence that your new family member has the best possible start in life.

We work closely with our veterinarian and use reputable testing laboratories to screen for breed-specific conditions. All test results are available upon request.

Health Testing Photo

Our Testing Protocol

Every breeding dog undergoes the following comprehensive health screenings

Hip & Elbow Evaluation

X-rays evaluated by OFA (Orthopedic Foundation for Animals) to screen for hip and elbow dysplasia.

  • OFA Hip Certification
  • OFA Elbow Certification
  • PennHIP evaluation available

Eye Certification

Annual eye exams by a board-certified veterinary ophthalmologist.

  • CAER (OFA Eye) Certification
  • Progressive Retinal Atrophy (PRA)
  • Cataracts screening

Cardiac Evaluation

Heart health screening by a board-certified veterinary cardiologist.

  • OFA Cardiac Certification
  • Echocardiogram
  • Auscultation exam

Genetic Testing

Comprehensive DNA testing for breed-specific genetic conditions.

  • von Willebrand Disease (vWD)
  • Degenerative Myelopathy (DM)
  • Exercise-Induced Collapse (EIC)
  • Neonatal Encephalopathy

Patellar Evaluation

Knee examination to screen for luxating patella.

  • OFA Patella Certification
  • Physical examination
  • Grade assessment

Additional Screenings

Breed-specific tests based on the dog's lineage.

  • Thyroid Function
  • Coat Color Genetics
  • Size Prediction Markers

Our Commitment

Verified Results

All test results are verified and available for review upon request.

Annual Testing

Eye and cardiac exams are repeated annually to monitor ongoing health.

Breeding Standards

Only dogs that pass all health screenings enter our breeding program.

Full Transparency

Health records and certifications shared with all puppy families.
