Health Testing

Our commitment to breeding healthy, happy puppies through comprehensive health testing.

Why Health Testing Matters

At SACC Doodles, we believe that responsible breeding starts with comprehensive health testing. Before any of our dogs enter our breeding program, they undergo extensive health screenings to ensure they are free from genetic conditions that could be passed on to their puppies.

Our commitment to health testing means that when you bring home a SACC Doodle puppy, you can have confidence that your new family member has the best possible start in life.

Our Testing Protocol

Every breeding dog undergoes comprehensive health screenings

Hip & Elbow Evaluation

OFA certified X-rays to screen for hip and elbow dysplasia.

Eye Certification

Annual eye exams by a board-certified veterinary ophthalmologist.

Cardiac Evaluation

Heart health screening by a board-certified veterinary cardiologist.

Genetic Testing

Comprehensive DNA testing for breed-specific genetic conditions.

Patellar Evaluation

Knee examination to screen for luxating patella.

Additional Screenings

Breed-specific tests based on the dog's lineage.

Health Guarantee

Every SACC Doodles puppy comes with a comprehensive health guarantee. We stand behind the health of our puppies because we've done the work upfront to ensure they're starting life on the right paw.
