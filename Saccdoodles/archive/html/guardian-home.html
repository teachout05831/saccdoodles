What is a Guardian Home?

A Guardian Home is a unique partnership between our breeding program and loving families like yours. Simply put: our dogs to breed, yours to love!

Instead of keeping all our breeding dogs in a kennel environment, we place them with carefully selected families who raise them as their own beloved pets. Your guardian dog lives with you full-time, sleeps in your bed, goes on family adventures, and receives all the love and attention they deserve.

In return, we ask that you bring your dog back to us periodically for breeding purposes. Once the breeding program is complete, your guardian dog is spayed/neutered and becomes 100% yours forever.

This arrangement ensures our dogs live their best lives in real homes while still contributing to our breeding program. It's truly a win-win for everyone, especially the dogs!

Why Become a Guardian Home?

Enjoy incredible benefits while giving a dog the loving home they deserve

Free Premium Puppy

Receive a "pick of the litter" quality puppy at no cost. These are the same exceptional puppies we typically sell for thousands of dollars, selected for their outstanding health, temperament, and breed characteristics.

Breeding Costs Covered

We cover all breeding-related expenses including health testing, genetic screenings, reproductive veterinary care, and spay/neuter surgery when the program is complete. You only pay for routine care like food and regular vet visits.

Ongoing Support

Join our extended SACC Doodles family! You'll have direct access to us for any questions about training, health, or care. We're invested in your success and your dog's happiness for life.

Health Guaranteed

Our guardian dogs receive comprehensive health testing and come with full health guarantees. You're getting a dog with documented excellent genetics and health clearances that most pet owners never have access to.

Yours Forever

After completing the breeding program (typically 3-4 litters for females or until age 6 for males), your dog is spayed/neutered and becomes 100% yours with no strings attached. The best part? You've had them since puppyhood!

Minimal Time Away

Your dog lives with you 95% of the time! Females visit us briefly for breeding and whelping (having puppies), while males may visit just a few times per year. The rest of the time, they're home with you.

How It Works

A simple process to welcome a guardian dog into your family

1

Apply & Interview

Fill out our application below. We'll review it and schedule a call or visit to get to know you, your family, and your living situation. We want to make sure it's a great fit for both you and the dog!

2

Match & Placement

Once approved, we'll match you with the perfect puppy based on your lifestyle and preferences. You'll sign a guardian agreement and take your new family member home!

3

Raise & Love

Enjoy raising your puppy! Provide training, socialization, regular vet care, and lots of love. We're always here to help with any questions along the way.

4

Breeding Period

For females: When she goes into heat (around 2 years old), you'll bring her to us for breeding. She'll stay with us during whelping (about 8 weeks) then return home to you.

For males: You'll bring him to us a few times during each breeding cycle. He'll be home with you the vast majority of the time.

5

Retirement & Forever Yours

After completing the program, we'll spay/neuter your dog at our expense. The co-ownership ends, and your guardian dog is officially 100% yours to love for the rest of their life!

Guardian Home Requirements

We're looking for committed families who meet the following criteria

Suitable Living Space

A home with a securely fenced yard is preferred. The dog must live indoors as a family pet, not kept outside.

Local to Our Area

Must live within reasonable driving distance for breeding visits and health appointments. We need to be able to coordinate easily.

Dog Experience

Prior experience owning and caring for dogs. You should be comfortable with training, grooming, and basic dog care.

Commitment to Care

Provide quality food, regular grooming, routine veterinary care, and keep up with vaccinations and preventatives.

Open Communication

Maintain regular contact with us, share photos/updates, and promptly communicate any health concerns or changes.

Flexible Schedule

Ability to coordinate vet appointments, breeding visits, and (for females) whelping periods with our schedule.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long is the guardian commitment?

For females, typically until they've had 3-4 litters (usually around 4-5 years). For males, until around age 6. After completing the program, your dog is spayed/neutered and becomes 100% yours.

How much time will my dog be away from me?

Your dog lives with you about 95% of the time! Females are only away during breeding and whelping periods (about 8-10 weeks per litter). Males may visit for just a few days at a time during breeding.

What costs am I responsible for?

You cover routine care: quality food, regular grooming, standard vet visits, vaccinations, and preventatives. We cover all breeding-related costs including health testing, reproductive care, and the final spay/neuter surgery.

Can I travel or go on vacation?

Yes! You can travel and vacation as you would with any dog. Just coordinate with us around breeding times and arrange appropriate care for your dog while you're away.

What if my circumstances change?

Life happens! If you can no longer fulfill the guardian commitment, contact us immediately. We'll work together to find the best solution for everyone, especially the dog.

Do I get to keep any puppies?

While the puppies belong to our breeding program, guardian families often receive discounts on puppies from their dog's litters if they want to add another furry family member!
